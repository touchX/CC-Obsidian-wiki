## 📋 Prompt Structure Framework

### Subject Description Layer

- Primary subject definition
- Subject details (expressions, poses, textures)
- Subject interaction with environment
- Scale and proportion specifications

### Environment & Setting Layer

- Location type (studio, outdoor, urban)
- Environmental details and textures
- Background treatment
- Atmospheric conditions

### Lighting Specification Layer

- Light source (natural or artificial)
- Light direction (front, side, back, Rembrandt)
- Light quality (hard/soft, diffused, volumetric)
- Color temperature specifications

### Technical Photography Layer

- Camera perspective and angles
- Focal length effects
- Depth of field specifications
- Exposure style (high key, low key, HDR)

## 🎨 Genre-Specific Patterns

### Portrait Photography

Cinematic lighting, 85mm lens, shallow depth of field, bokeh effects.

### Product Photography

Hero shots, lifestyle, detail shots, gradient backdrops.

### Landscape Photography

Wide angle, deep focus, dramatic lighting, color grading.

### Fashion Photography

Editorial poses, dramatic lighting, magazine aesthetic.

## 🚀 Platform-Specific Optimization

- **Midjourney**: Parameter usage, multi-prompt weighting
- **DALL-E**: Natural language optimization
- **Stable Diffusion**: Token weighting, LoRA integration
- **Flux**: Detailed natural language, photorealistic emphasis

## 📷 Technical Photography Translation

Convert photography knowledge into prompt language:

- Aperture and focal length specifications
- Lighting setups (golden hour to studio)
- Post-processing aesthetics and color grading
- Reference photographers and techniques
